AATROX

Tips

  • Use first Q to poke enemies in lane. If you do not use all 3 charges, your Q cooldown will become shorter.
  • Trade during your passive windows. Your empowered auto gives you more damage and heals you.
  • You excel in extended fights due to the sustain from your ultimate.

Counters

  • Don't trade with him when he has his passive ready. His passive makes his next atack do bonus damage and heals him.
  • Dodge his Qs. Don't move in straight lines. Most times, moving towards Aatrox when he does his first Q will make him miss.
  • If you get hit by his W, the quickest way out of the zone is sideways.
  • Anti-healing like grievous wounds is a must buy when facing Aatrox. Buy healing reduction early like:
    • Executioner's Culling into Mortal Reminder/Chempunk Chainsword(AD champs)
    • Oblivion Orb into Morellonomicon (AD champs)
  • CC and burst him before he heals.
  • Avoid fighting or kite Aatrox when he ults and you can reinitiate when it runs out. When he ults he gets:
    • Movespeed boost
    • Healing on everything

AHRI

Tips

  • When chasing, hold on to your Charm when the enemy starts moving side to side, you can use this time instead to auto them or walk up and close up the gap.

Counters

  • Keep in mind of Ahri's ultimate cooldown, which starts when she uses it for the first time. Once she gets a few resets and spaces out her dashes, her ultimate might already be up in few dozen seconds.
  • During the laning phase, if you have an important ability that you need to land, note that Ahri's W (with the three orbs around her) gives her bonus movement speed.
  • Ahri receives an additional ult charge upon every kill, so if its possible to completely avoid skirmishing when she ults, do so.
  • Ahri's damage early on heavily relies on landing her skillshots (Q and E). As the game progresses, do not underestimate her W damage, which takes no skill and is extremely mana friendly and spammable.
  • Restricting Ahri's movement can be very limiting for her. An example is cageing her with Veigar's E.

AKALI

Tips

  • You can R1 do dodge abilites like malphite R and Zac's jump when using it on them.
  • You can go for quick trades with using E and minions. Dash forward with E1 and hit a minion, trade, then E2 to jump back.

Counters

  • Ways to reveal Akali in her shroud (W):
    • Pink wards do not reveal Akali when she is in her shroud, but Oracle Lens does.
    • Champions with reveals can also catch her out (Lee Sin's Q or Galio's E)
    • Ignite also reveals enemies so you could apply it to her before she goes invisible again.
  • After Akali uses the first part of her ultimate, she can only recast it after 2.5 seconds have passed. As long as you see the first part go off, you will have enough time to prepare for the next part of her execution.
  • Fight in open spaces (teamfights as well). Akali's shroud and mobility become much stronger in tight spaces.
  • Akali's mobility allow her to easily dodge abilities. Your team should have some point and click hard CC to lock her down, followed by burst damage to quickly kill her.

AKSHAN

Tips

  • Cancelling the second auto gives you bonus movement speed, use it to chase enemies down.
  • After killing a minion, your second shot will hit the closest enemy.
  • You won't get slowed while charging your R, but firing it makes you stand in place. You can time your E such that you swing during the firing animation (Throw E during R1 charging, reactivate E right before you reactivate R2)
  • Casting abilities reveal you from W's stealth, but summoner spells won't.

Counters

  • Akshan can only stay invisible when he is basically touching a wall. If he leaves the wall, he will lose the invisibility after 2 seconds.
  • Control wards reveal Akshan. Place them wisely.
  • Respect his early burst. Akshan has great trading ability with his passive shield and double autos.
  • Punish his cooldowns. His E and Q have quite long cooldowns early on. If he wastes them and misses, engage immediately.
  • His Boomerang (Q) goes a further distance upon every enemy unit hit. So mind the minions and avoid standing behind wave.
  • Akshan's ultimate gets blocked by minions, allies, jungle monsters, and towers.
  • Don't give him free revive resets. Fight him first or deny him resets during skirmishes.
  • Hard CCs cancel his E.

ALISTAR

Tips

  • Q flash does not work. You need to flash then Q.
  • Use your ultimate in moments when you think you will get bursted or will be taking the most damage, not when you are low HP.
  • You can Headbutt (W) people over walls, use this to temporarily remove enemies from a fight.
  • Be creative with your Headbutt (W), you can use it deny enemy cannons, zoning specific enemies etc, but obviously use it carefully because it makes you vulnerable after.

Counters

  • Alistar's ultimate does not reduce the true damage, so items like Kraken Slayer can destroy him.
  • Avoid standing between a wall and Alistar. If you do, he can headbutt (W) you into the wall first, then stagger the CC with his Pulverize (Q).
  • Punish him when his cooldowns are down. Alistar is useless after W-Q combo.
  • You can consider disengaging and waiting out his ultimate.
  • Alistar is melee and has a kit that forces him to commit to a fight. Champions with good poke can give him a hard time by chunking him down before he can engage.
  • Spread out during fights to not give him a penta CC.
  • His headbutt (W) does not follow you when you flash away during its animation.

AMBESSA

Tips

  • You can use tiamat between during E without losing momentum.
  • Your Q2 has a very long window, save it for a higher likelihood of landing it.
  • If you cast Q2 immediately after you Ultimate (without clicking the ground after Ultimate), it is guaranteed to hit.

Counters

  • Ambessa's cooldowns are extremely high early on so punish her after she does her combos.
  • Ambessa's passive gives her empowered autos are abilities which refund energy. Don't let her stack multiple empowered hits.
  • Respect her level 2, 3 and 6 powerspikes.

AMUMU

Tips

  • If your max E, you take no damage from minions. As a support, you can hold 20 minions for the rest of the game.
  • You can Ult flash to animation cancel your Ultimate.
  • You can Q onto a tank, then Ult flash their backline.
  • You can flash when you are flying towards an enemy. Use this to reposition for a better Ultimate and avoid telegraphing yourself for enemy CC.
  • When ganking, just force enemies to waste their movement abilities or flash. Q immediately since you also have a second Q charge.

Counters

  • Amumu' kit doesn't allow him to have many tricks up his sleeve (except flash Q or flash Ult).
  • Dodging Amumu's Bandage Toss (Q) is the only thing you need to focus on (apart from his flash).
  • Do not clump in fights and give him that penta CC.
  • Basically, track his flash cooldowns.

ANIVIA

Tips

  • Walls under people count as a knockup. You can stop channeling spells like Katarina or Shen ultimates and synergies with Yasuo's. You can even interrupt Pyke's Q charge up. Using it on scuttle can remove it shield if you don't have Q.
  • Use Ultimate, then wall the enemies to keep them in it for longer. Hold onto your Q as your Ultimate is already making them chilled and allowing you buffed E damage.
  • Zhonya's won't turn your Ultimate off.
  • Wall your teammates to gain tear stacks.

Counters

  • Anivia's Q is a major part of her combo. Dodging it removes a lot of kill pressure since getting chilled by her Q or Ultimate means more damage from frostbite (E).
  • Remember about her passive Rebirth and don't get baited.
  • Anivia has mana problems early on if she uses abilities too frequently.
  • Do not clump in fights and give her a 5 man ultimate.
  • Punish her in lane before she gets items.

AURELION SOL

Tips

  • Your Q gets interrupted by hard CC.
  • Not only does your E slow, but it also executes enemies below 5% (or more due to stardust stacks). The execution does not apply to Epic monsters.
  • Use W wisely. Place your W lines across terrain rather than directly on the enemy team. You W resets upon every kill so focus on 1 or 2 targets at a time.
  • Your E is an amazing zoning tool. Place it in chokepoints and forget about it.
  • Do not underestimate your Q damage early on. Your Q cooldown resets when you use W and after W ends. In lane, you can Q the enemy, W then Q, then continuing to Q the enemy after W ends for a potential kill. This is especially good for immobile enemies (after wasting their CC) or enemies without CC.
  • If you are not confident in killing the laner, just chill and stack. You become a monster with stacks and a few items.

Counters

  • Respect his level 1 trading capability.
  • Champion with stuns can deny Sol's Starsurge (Q) during lane.
  • Sol's Q gives him stardust stacks from enemy champions. Avoid giving him free stacks by just standing in his face.
  • Do not stay in his Singularity (E) for too long, he gets a stardust stack for every second an enemy champion stands in it.
  • If the game gets too late and the enemy team has good peeling capabilites, then the game will be extremely hard for you unless they throw.

AZIR

Tips

  • The biggest challenge for Azir is soldier placement. Missing a W or Q makes you lose out on a lot of damage.
  • Hold your Ultimate. Don't force a Shurima Shuffle and die early in the fight, you can use it defensively. Your main source of damage is still from your soldiers.

Counters

BARD

Tips

  • Only three healing shrines can exist on the map at any point in time. You can place a fourth directly under an ally to prevent one of the previous three to disappear.
  • You can ult people that are in your portal. They will appear in stasis after exiting the portal.
  • Chimes give you movement speed when you pick them up. Try saving some chimes that spawn in the lane to use them for engage/disengage.
  • You can Magical Journey (W) from fountain to side inhibitors using the walls.
  • You can clear waves really fast in the late game with meeps.
  • Don't be afraid to use healing shrines on yourself as you are collecting chimes throughout the jungle, chimes give you mana.
  • if for some reason your team already has enough sustain, you can place your healing shrines in bushes around the map. If they get destroyed, then an enemy has gone there and stepped on it.

Counters

  • Banshee's Veil isn't very good against Bard's stun. It has two procs, the first is the damage, the second is when it hits a second object or a wall.
  • Punish his lack of damage in the early game.

Tips

  • Don't spam W.
  • The better combo is to W and run up to the enemy to Power Fist (E) them, then hooking to stagger your CC.

Counters

BRAND

Tips

  • You proc your passive when you land 3 abilities on an enemy. Which means it really is only possible to proc it at level 3 onwards.
  • Be patient with your Q.
  • when poking enemies in lane with W, try hitting the caster minions at the back as well. This way, you can use E on the minions and it will bounce to the enemy, rather than having to walk up and E the enemy instead.
  • At close to max range, you can E after throwing out Q to surprise enemies with a stun.
  • Your Ultimate has the longest range in your entire kit.

Counters

  • Brand's damage is mainly percent health, so MR helps more than HP when building against him.

Counters
